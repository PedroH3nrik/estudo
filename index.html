Origin

The Soul of Carnaíba

Nestled in the rugged terrains of Bahia, Carnaíba is more than a mining district — it is the cradle of Brazil's most extraordinary emeralds. For generations, families have extracted these gems with respect for the land, preserving both tradition and the environment.

We do not romanticize the hardship of mining. We honor it. Every emerald from Henry Pietro carries the authentic story of its origin: the specific vein, the artisan who extracted it, and the journey it undertook from rough crystal to polished masterpiece.
